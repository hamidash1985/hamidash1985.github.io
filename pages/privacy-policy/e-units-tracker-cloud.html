1. Information We Collect

  • The App allows you to manually enter electricity meter readings.
  • These readings, along with related information (such as dates and calculated results), are stored securely in the cloud.
  • Storage and access are linked to an anonymous device identifier generated by the App. No personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email, location, or contact details is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • The stored data is used solely for calculation purposes (to determine electricity consumption over a period of time).
  • Data is not used for advertising, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
  • The App does not run background data collection beyond what is necessary for secure cloud storage.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely on a cloud service using an anonymous device ID.
  • Reasonable measures are taken to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
  • While the App strives to protect your information, no method of storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • The App does not share, sell, rent, or disclose your information to third parties.
  • No third-party analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs are integrated into the App.
  • Your usage remains private and limited to the anonymous device ID.

5. User Responsibility

  • You are responsible for ensuring your device is secure and has a reliable internet connection for syncing with the cloud.
  • If your device is lost, reset, or replaced, access to your previous data depends on the anonymous device ID. Recovery may not be possible if the identifier is lost.

6. Disclaimer of Liability

  • The App is provided “as is”, without warranties of any kind.
  • The developer shall not be held liable for:
    • Data loss, corruption, or deletion on the cloud service.
    • Incorrect calculations due to wrong input.
    • Any damages, financial or otherwise, arising from use of the App.
  • By using the App, you agree that the developer provides no warranty and holds no obligation for any unfortunate event linked to the App’s use or cloud storage.

7. Data Retention

  • Data stored in the cloud is retained as long as the App remains installed and actively used on your device.
  • If you uninstall the App, your data may be permanently deleted from the cloud after a reasonable period of inactivity.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the developer (see Contact section below).

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not target children and does not knowingly collect any information from users under the age of 13. Since the App collects no personal information, it is safe for general use.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

The developer may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be posted on the App’s Google Play Store listing or within the App itself. Continued use of the App after policy changes implies acceptance of the revised terms.
