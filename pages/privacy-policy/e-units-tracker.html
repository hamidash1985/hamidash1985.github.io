1. Information We Collect

  • The App allows you to manually enter electricity meter readings.
  • These readings are stored only on your device’s internal storage.
  • The App may also store supporting information, such as dates of readings and calculated results (total units consumed).
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email, location, or contact details is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • The stored data is used solely for calculation purposes (to determine electricity consumption over a period of time).
  • Data is not used for advertising, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
  • The App does not run background data collection processes.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is saved locally on your device.
  • The App does not use cloud storage, external servers, or online backups.
  • Since data never leaves your device, you have full control over your information.
  • We recommend that you maintain regular backups of your device to avoid accidental data loss.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • The App does not share, sell, rent, or disclose any information to third parties.
  • No third-party analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs are integrated into the App.
  • Your usage remains completely private.

5. User Responsibility

  • If your device is lost, stolen, or damaged, the developer cannot recover your data.
  • The developer does not provide cloud backup, data migration, or data restoration services.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not target children and does not knowingly collect any information from users under the age of 13. Since the App contains no external data collection, it is safe for general use.
